Know Your Rights

🏠
Habitability

Your landlord must keep the unit safe and livable. Document every repair request in writing.

💰
Security Deposit

In MN, landlords must return your deposit within 21 days of move-out or provide an itemized list.

📋
Eviction Notice

You must receive written notice before any eviction filing. Keep every notice you receive.

🔒
Retaliation

It is illegal for a landlord to evict you for reporting code violations or organizing with neighbors.
